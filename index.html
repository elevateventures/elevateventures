Privacy Policy


Elevate Ventures take your privacy seriously. Please read the following to learn more about our privacy policy.

What This Privacy Policy Covers This policy covers how Elevate Ventures treat personal information that Elevate Ventures collect and receive, including information related to your past use of Elevate Ventures products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable like your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. This policy does not apply to the practices of companies that Elevate Ventures do not own or control, or to people that Elevate Ventures do not employ or manage.

Collection and Use


General Elevate Ventures collect personal information when you register with Elevate Ventures, when you use Elevate Ventures products or services, when you visit Elevate Ventures pages or the pages of certain Elevate Ventures partners, and when you enter promotions. Elevate Ventures may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies. When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender, zip code, occupation, industry, and personal interests. Once you register with Elevate Ventures and sign in to our services, you may not be anonymous to us. Elevate Ventures may collect information about your transactions with us. Elevate Ventures automatically receive and record information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, Elevate Ventures cookie information, and the page you request. Elevate Ventures use information for the following general purposes: to customize the content you see, fulfil your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal audit.

Children


Elevate Ventures will not contact children under age 13 about special offers or for marketing purposes without a parent’s permission. Elevate Ventures do not ask a child under age 13 for more personal information, as a condition of participation, than is reasonably necessary to participate in a given activity or promotion.

Information Sharing and Disclosure


Elevate Ventures do not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you’ve requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances: We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with Elevate Ventures under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help Elevate Ventures communicate with you about offers from Elevate Ventures. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information. We have a parent’s permission to share the information if the user is a child under age 13; We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of Elevate Ventures terms of use, or as otherwise required by law. We transfer information about you if Elevate Ventures is acquired by or merged with another organization. In this event, Elevate Ventures will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Cookies


Elevate Ventures may set and access Elevate Ventures cookies on your computer.

Confidentiality and Security


We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs. We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect personal information about you. Your Elevate Ventures Account Information is password-protected. In certain areas Elevate Ventures use industry-standard SSL-encryption to protect data transmissions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy


Elevate Ventures may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your Elevate Ventures account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
